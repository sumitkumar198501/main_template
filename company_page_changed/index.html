About DIZENVI

DIZENVI is a fast growing technology company that is taking a sincere step towards transforming and accelerating digitization of several tasks that are currently done manually in small and medium sized enterprises (SMEs).

Our mission is to provide a personalized digital ecosystem to SMEs in India that enhances their experience in managing core business operations, thereby optimizing their revenue streams.
